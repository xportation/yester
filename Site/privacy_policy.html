Privacy Policy

No personally identifiable information is stored on your device or sent back regarding your use of the application. Any user names or passwords necessary for application 'share' features are not stored or used other than to share the content.

Application usage information is tracked using Google Analytics for Android, in accordance with their privacy policy, to allow us to determine which features are most popular, how they are being used, and how we can improve the applications.

If you have any questions regarding the privacy policy, contact us at xportation.dev@gmail.com.
